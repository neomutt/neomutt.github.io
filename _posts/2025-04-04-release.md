## :book: Notes

This is (mostly) a Bug-Fix Release.

Most of other changes are improving the startup/help, e.g.

- `neomutt -h       # Clearer help`
- `neomutt -h send  # Specific help with examples`
- `neomutt -DD      # Dump different (changed) config`
